## Objective

Transform EVENTS from "runtime stable" to **full business feature**, guaranteeing:

- Event creation and editing
- Controlled publishing and unpublishing
- Category management
- Bracket generation and publishing
- Public and administrative views
- Zero regression on existing flows
- Total determinism (testable and predictable)

## Scope

### What's Included

1. **Event CRUD (Admin)**
   - Create event (DRAFT)
   - Edit event (DRAFT only)
   - Publish event (DRAFT → PUBLISHED)
   - Cancel event (PUBLISHED → CANCELLED)
   - Archive event (CANCELLED → ARCHIVED)

2. **Event Categories**
   - Create/edit categories in DRAFT events
   - READ-ONLY after publication

3. **Event Brackets**
   - Generate bracket from categories
   - Regenerate while DRAFT/GENERATED
   - Publish bracket
   - Lock after event starts

4. **Event Visibility**
   - `/events` — public listing
   - `/events/:id` — public details
   - `/admin/events` — administrative management
   - `/admin/events/:id` — full management

### What's Excluded

❌ AUTH modifications
❌ BILLING modifications
❌ MEMBERSHIP modifications
❌ Automatic redirects
❌ Implicit mutations
❌ Non-deterministic time

| From | To |
|------|-----|

### Bracket Status Transitions

| From | To |
|------|-----|
| DRAFT | GENERATED |
| GENERATED | PUBLISHED, DRAFT |
| PUBLISHED | LOCKED |
| LOCKED | (terminal) |

## Business Rules

### Event Editing

- ✅ DRAFT events are editable
- ❌ PUBLISHED events are READ-ONLY
- ❌ Events cannot be deleted (soft state only)

### Category Management

- ✅ Categories can be created/edited in DRAFT events
- ❌ Categories are READ-ONLY after publication

### Bracket Management

- ✅ Brackets can be generated/regenerated while DRAFT/GENERATED
- ❌ PUBLISHED brackets are immutable
- ❌ LOCKED brackets cannot be modified

### Visibility

- ✅ PUBLISHED + is_public events are publicly visible
- ❌ DRAFT events are admin-only
- ⚠️ CANCELLED events show warning
